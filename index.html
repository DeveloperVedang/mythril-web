Mythril Host

Mythril Host is a platform providing both free and paid hosting services tailored for Minecraft servers and Discord bots. The service emphasizes powerful, 24/7 uptime, ensuring your game servers and bots remain online and functional around the clock.

These plans are ideal for larger communities or more demanding applications, ensuring optimal performance and minimal latency.
